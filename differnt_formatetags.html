Make a cup of tea with just a few steps

ingredients


        1 cup water
         1 tea bag
   1 teaspoon sugar(optional).
    1/2 cup milk(optional)
   1 slice of lemons(optional) 



Instrutions


        1.Boil water
   2.place tea bag in cup.
3.Pour hot water over the tea.
   4. steep for 3-5 minutes.
5.Add sugar or honey if you like
  6.Add milk if you like
   7.Add lemon if desired.
      8.Enjoy your tea!
